MM Dance Studio

MM Dance Studio began in a basement studio in 2015. Since then, it has continued to grow and welcome students of all ages, backgrounds and experience levels. Nowadays, studio became popular because of artists, choreographies for other artists and dancing performances in several festivals and concerts, We have a lot of partners, also from South Korea.

Located in Almaty, Kazakhstan, MM’s facility holds one fully equipped studio and a full-time staff who speaks either Kazakh or Russian or English. MM invites you to explore your creative potential and find a community among mentors and peers that never cease to INSPIRE MILLIONS.

Dance studio is located behind the hotel Kazakhstan. At the crossinf of Qarmysova and Qurmangazy streets.
Address: Qarmysova 82




Classes Information
In-Person Class MM’s physical location holds up to 6 classes a day. Classes can be either booked on the MM website or through the MM DANCE STUDIO app.
Online Class Online classes are offered on a variety of platforms, including on the MM Dance Studio app and on MM’s YouTube channel. Please check the class schedule for more details.
