Fairy Tail
Fairy Tail is a shonen manga by Hiro Mashima. The manga was published in Kodansha Weekly Shonen Magazine from August 2006 to July 2017. The chapters were later compiled into 63 volumes of tancobones. The story tells the adventures of Natsu Dragnila, a member of the popular wizard guild. The manga was adapted into an anime series produced by A-1 Pictures, Dentsu Inc., Satelight, Bridge and CloverWorks, which aired from October 2009 to March 2013. The second season aired from April 2014 to March 2016. The third and final season aired from October 2018 to September 2019.

But guilds are not just a way to earn money. For many mages it is also a family. At the center of the action is the continent's most reckless guild, Fairy Tail, with its glorious history and the strongest mages, as well as the notorious destroyers of anything and everything.
